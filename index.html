Project Details

Topic: Provincial government digital attention and corporate financial constraints in VIETNAM: the role of digitalization and ESG

Overview: This independent research explores the influence of provincial government digital attention (PGDA) on corporate financial constraints, highlighting the mediating role of digitalization and moderating role of ESG.

Methodology & Execution:

  • Data Collection (Python): Programmed Python scripts to perform web scraping. Extracted and scanned official documents from the portals of Departments of Science and Technology across 47 provinces to count digital transformation-related keywords for the PGDA variable. Simultaneously scanned annual reports of 400 listed companies for the DIG variable.
  • Data Processing (MS Excel): Aggregated, cleaned, and processed the raw scraped data to generate descriptive statistics.
  • Econometric Analysis (Stata): Utilized Stata to analyze financial metrics and run advanced econometric models, including Generalized Method of Moments (System-GMM) and Two-Stage Least Squares (2SLS), ensuring robustness in identifying causation, mediation, and moderation effects.
